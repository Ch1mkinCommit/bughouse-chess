Privacy policy

This privacy notice describes how we process your information when you use bughouse.pro website.

Please reach out to contact.bughousepro@gmail.com if you have any questions.

What information do we collect?

Personal information provided by you. This includes your username, password and email address. If you choose to log in with Google, we also receive your public profile information such as name and profile picture.

Automatically collected information. This includes service usage data, performance statistics and basic information about your system (such as your OS and browser version). It also includes information that allows us to estimate your location (such as IP address and timezone), but it does not include precise location information (such as GPS data).

Please note that your game history is considered public information and is out of scope of this policy.

How do we use your information?

We use your information to provide services to you and to improve our systems. We do not use your data for advertising.

With whom do we share your information?

We may share your data with Google for the following purposes:

  • Allowing you to sign in with Google if you so choose;
  • Web analytics via Google Analytics.

We do not share your data with other third parties.

How long do we keep your information?

We keep your information as long as it is required in order to provide our services.

When we have no ongoing business need to process your personal information, we will delete or anonymize such information, or, if this is not feasible (for example, because your information is stored in backup archives), then we isolate your information from further processing until a deletion is possible.

How can you update or delete your information?

Please go to your account settings in order to update your account information or delete your account.

Do we make updates to this policy?

Yes, we may update this policy. You can always find the latest policy version at bughouse.pro/privacy-policy.html
